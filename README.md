# VANCE Garden

## Never Come Back To A Pile Of Work Again.

VANCE Garden is a commission-neutral operational relief platform that identifies issues, prepares action plans, tracks improvements, and helps organizations recover capacity through a simple review-and-approve workflow.

Garden Access includes a monthly capacity goal of up to 40 hours per user. Based on adoption, workflow utilization, and organizational participation, VANCE helps reduce administrative friction, simplify required work, and create more time for value-added activities.

VANCE never participates in commissions, transaction proceeds, or deal compensation.

Garden Access is provided at no cost.

Simply review the recommendations, approve or modify the workflow, and continue focusing on the work that matters most.
